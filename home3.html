About W3 Grads

W3 Grads is a premier online learning platform dedicated to bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements. Our mission is to empower students and professionals with practical, job-ready skills through comprehensive courses, hands-on projects, and expert mentorship.

Founded in 2015, we've helped over 50,000 learners transform their careers in fields like web development, data science, cloud computing, and more. Our industry-aligned curriculum is designed by experts from top tech companies.
