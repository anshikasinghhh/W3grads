Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our placement assistance program.

Is placement guaranteed?

We provide 100% placement assistance, which includes preparing you for interviews and connecting you with hiring partners. While we cannot guarantee a job, our dedicated support significantly increases your chances of getting hired. Your success ultimately depends on your performance in the interviews.

What is the average salary package of placed students?

The salary package depends on various factors like the company, job role, and the candidate's skills. However, our graduates have consistently secured competitive packages in the industry, ranging from entry-level to advanced positions.

Do I have to pay extra for placement assistance?

Our placement assistance program is an integral part of our professional courses and is included in the course fee. There are no hidden or extra charges for our placement support services.
